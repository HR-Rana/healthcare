    Absolutely, and you will feel much better when you do. This program is created to meet people where they currently are and build from there. For beginners, it will give them easy, very short workouts to wake up the muscles and the cardiovascular system, but even if you’ve been working out for a while, you’ll benefit from it.

The at-home circuit training alone will give results, however.

    Can diabetics do this program?

    Yes, diabetics can benefit greatly from utilizing this program. The mental, emotional and spiritual lessons will give added power and self-confidence. Their physical training will help to gain strength, stamina, endurance, flexibility and balance. With proper exercise and nutrition, anyone can lose weight if that is their goal.
